Success is not about motivation.

It’s about consistency.

## 🔥 The Truth

Motivation comes and goes.
Discipline stays.

## ⚡ Daily Wins

- 1 hour of learning
- 1 small improvement
- 1 step forward

## 📈 Growth Formula

Consistency > Talent

## 💡 Example

Instead of:
- Studying 10 hours once

Do:
- Study 1 hour daily

## 🧠 Final Thought

Small steps repeated daily create massive results.
