We call it automation.

But let’s be honest.

- Cron jobs ≠ intelligence  
- Alerts ≠ solutions  
- Scaling ≠ fixing  

Most systems today are just **if-else scripts at scale**.

They don’t understand context. 
They don’t learn. 
They don’t adapt.

They just react.

And when things go wrong?

They either:
- Spam alerts  
- Or throw more CPU at the problem  

Both are expensive.  
Neither is smart.

## 🏗️ Architecture: How It Actually Works

### 🔹 1. Data Ingestion Layer

### 🔹 2. Intelligence Layer (Brain)
- Self-learning anomaly detection
- Pattern recognition (not static thresholds)
- Context-aware decision engine

### 🔹 3. Decision Engine
- Evaluates risk before action  
- Selects safest and most optimal fix  
- Prevents repetitive or harmful actions  

### 🔹 4. Execution Layer
- FastAPI-driven control system  
- Direct interaction with servers (SSH/APIs)

### 🔹 5. Feedback Loop
- Observes outcome of actions  
- Learns from success/failure  
- Improves future decisions
